# Practical information

## Times & venue
Each day starts at 10:00 and ends at 17:00. Around 12:30, lunch is provided.

The summer school takes place at *Bolognalaan 101, room 2.049*, on the Utrecht Science Park. See [this link](https://www.uu.nl/en/bolognalaan-101) for accesibility, facilities, and directions.

## Contact
For any questions, please contact [Jelte van Boheemen](mailto:j.vanboheemen@uu.nl) or [Mees van Stiphout](mailto:m.vanstiphout@uu.nl).
