# Final presentation

Your final presentation will be an opportunity to reflect on your own progress as a software developer this week, and how you will design and develop your digital solution to a research problem. This problem can be gathering your data, organizing them, analysing them, or presenting them. Or any other application that you can think of.

In 5 minutes, outline your software solution:
 
 - Give a brief introduction to your research topic or application (1 min)
 - Outline the software that you will develop to carry out a part of your project (1 min)
 - Present a list of need-to-have features for your software (and optionally some nice-to-haves) and how you are planning to develop them (3 mins)
